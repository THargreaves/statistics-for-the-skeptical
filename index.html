Tim Hargreaves
In a society filled with fake news, misleading journalism, and propaganda, how can you be sure that stories you're told match up with reality? When economies are been transformed though the data revolution, how do you see through the haze and draw the right conclusions? A deep understanding of statistics is certainly not for everyone but understanding a few key concepts can prove integral for illuminating a world full of uncertainty. This talk introduces a handful of these important ideas using real-world examples centred around the objectives of international development. With the aid of discussion, visualisations, and anecdotes, perhaps you can learn to see the challenges the word faces with a newfound skeptisim.
